At the homepage, there is a static sidebar at the left. Choosing the "Help" option will redirect you to this page. The remaining options are discussed below.

For students:

-The "Current courses" option at the sidebar on the homepage takes you to the courses you are enrolled in. From there you can navigate to any content related to your courses. Each page after you click any of your course will have a left menu to help you navigate through course related content.

-The "Announcements" option will direct you to all the announcements of all your courses that have been added. This is so you do not miss out on any of your deadline

For teachers:

-The "Current courses" option at the sidebar on the homepage takes you to the courses you are currently teaching. From there you can upload, add, remove content for any of your taught course.

-The "Announcements" option will direct you to a page where you can add the relevant announcements for your courses. This is so you can notify all your students about the upcoming deadlines
