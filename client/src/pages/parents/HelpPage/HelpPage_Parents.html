At the homepage, there is a static sidebar at the left. Choosing the "Help" option will redirect you to this page. The remaining options are discussed below.

For students:

-The "Students" option at the sidebar on the homepage takes you to a page that shows you all your children that are enrolled in the system. Clicking on a student button will direct you to the current courses that student is enrolled in. From here you can choose a course and see all the course related content of your child.

-The "Announcements" option at the sidebar on the homepage takes you to a page that shows you your children that are enrolled in the system. Clicking on a student button will direct you to a page that shows their relevant announcements for all their courses. This is to make parents aware of the pending graded components.

-The "Request Meeting" option at the sidebar on the homepage takes you to a page where parents can request a meeting to discuss the academic progress of their child.

-The user can log out by clicking on the right icon on the main header of the page
